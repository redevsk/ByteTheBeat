GAME MECHANICS

THE GAME WILL START WITH THREE (3) PLAYERS COMPETING PER SESSION.

STEP 1: SPIN THE WHEEL (YEAR CATEGORY SELECTION - 1980s, 1990s, 2000s, 2010s, 2020s OR 2025)

STEP 2: GAME ROUNDS

ROUND 1 - "GUESS THE SONG!"

Participants will listen to a short instrumental part of a song.

The first player to raise the answer paddle and correctly identify the song title earns 1 point.

ROUND 2 - "WHO'S THE SINGER?"

A short song clip will play.

The first to raise the paddle and correctly name the artist/singer earns 1 point.

ROUND 3 - "FINISH THE LYRICS!"

When the music stops, the first player to raise the paddle will continue singing or reciting the next line of the song, similar to The Singing Bee style.

Points are awarded for correct lyric continuation.
